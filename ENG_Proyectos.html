FINAL DEGREE PROJECT

BIM Model of the "Edificio Polivalente" from the University of Oviedo.

BIM, is a collaborative work methodology for the management of building projects or civil works through a digital model. Its objective is to cover the entire life cycle of the project, thus improving the creation and management of documentation, coordination between the different work groups, planning and budgeting, and all the other stages that exist throughout a project.

The main objective of this project is the 3D modeling of the building "Edificio Polivalente", located on the Gijón campus of the University of Oviedo, using the BIM methodology in order to show the possibilities that this new form of work offers to model existing buildings, not just with constructions.

In the following link, Modelo BIM del Edificio Polivalente del Campus Universitario de Gijón, you will find an interactive web viewer of the 3D model.
