April 2019

Madrid Half Marathon 2019

Second participation in a half marathon. After a year of hard preparation, the race goes wonderfully, achieving the objectives of improving the time achieved in Prague and falling below 1h 25min.

Also very happy to achieve 4th place in the sub23 category. A pity that third place escaped for just a second.

April 2018

Prague Half Marathon 2018

First half marathon in which I participate. The experience is unforgettable, in a beautiful city like Prague, with a crowded urban circuit.

Almost impossible to experience a greater feeling of satisfaction than I had when crossing the finish line and to verify that I had managed to improve the time that I had set as a goal of 1h30min. Finally, and despite the fact that the last 3 km seemed a bit long, I managed to finish in 1h 27min

April 2017

Farinato Race 2017 Madrid

For the first time, I am abandoning the asphalt races to enter the mud, the suffering and the effort of the obstacle races

Very fun adventure, in which one of my cousins accompanied me. Despite not having experience in this type of tests, we competed in the best way we could to get a good time and have a great time.
